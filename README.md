# 🛒 Superstore Sales & Revenue Analysis

## Overview
End-to-end business data analysis on 9,994 retail transactions
identifying revenue drivers, profit leakage and customer trends.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Dashboard)
- Tableau Public (Interactive Dashboard)

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle — Superstore Sales Dataset
- 9,994 rows, 21 columns
- 4 regions, 3 categories, 17 sub-categories

## Key Business Insights
- West region leads with $725,458 total revenue
- Technology has highest profit margin at 17.4%
- Furniture has lowest profit margin at only 2.5%
- Orders with discount above 20% generate average LOSS of $97 per order
- Consumer segment drives 50.6% of total revenue
- Tables sub-category is biggest loss maker at -$17,725 total loss
